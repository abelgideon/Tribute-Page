GM Magnus Carlsen

Here's Magnus Carlsen's World Championship matches:

  • 2013 - Defeated reigning champion Viswanathan Anand in Chennai, India, to become the World Chess Champion. Carlsen won with a score of 6.5-3.5, with no losses.
  • 2014 - Successfully defended his title against Viswanathan Anand in Sochi, Russia. Carlsen won with a score of 6.5-4.5.
  • 2016 - Faced Sergey Karjakin in New York City, USA. After a tied score of 6-6 in the classical games, Carlsen won the rapid tiebreaks 3-1.
  • 2018 - Defended his title against Fabiano Caruana in London, England. The match ended 6-6 in the classical games, but Carlsen dominated the rapid tiebreaks with a 3-0 victory.
  • 2021 - Faced Ian Nepomniachtchi in Dubai, UAE. Carlsen won convincingly with a score of 7.5-3.5, marking his fifth consecutive World Championship title defense.
